Lossless audio ripping from YouTube

Yes, it's actually possible. Here's how to do it.

(obvious note) This is only possible on videos that had... lossless audio uploaded to them. All of my non videogame music rips have lossless, so they work on them.

Note #2: Yes, YouTube caps audio quality at a certain point so you won't have a true lossless original but it's still acceptable.

1. Get a good downloader. In my opinion, cobalt.tools is the best out there for easy access.

2. Download in the highest quality possible. 4K+ should use AV1, but that's not widely supported on all videos so use VP9 instead.

3. Use a converter to convert it to a FLAC file. The higher the quality, the higher the bitrate (and size).

4. Profit
